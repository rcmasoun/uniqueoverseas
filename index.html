ABOUT US

Introduction to Unique Overseas India

Unique Overseas, established in 2009, is a trusted and reputed name in the field of overseas recruitment and global mobility services. With over a decade of industry experience, the company specializes in providing comprehensive manpower consultancy solutions to clients across Gulf countries, Europe, North America and other international markets. Unique Overseas connects skilled, semi-skilled, and professional talent with reputable employers in industries such as construction, healthcare, hospitality, manufacturing, retail, and engineering. In addition to recruitment services, the company also offers expert assistance in tourist visa processing, student visa guidance, and permanent residency (PR) applications, catering to individuals aiming for international travel, education, or long-term settlement abroad. Known for its ethical approach, transparent processing, and personalized client support, Unique Overseas has built a strong reputation as a reliable partner for both job seekers and global employers.
