Chander Mohan Sharma

MD (Managing Director)
Chander Mohan Sharma is the Managing Director of Unique Overseas, a trusted name in immigration and manpower consultancy since 2009. The company is officially registered with the Government of India, Ministry of External Affairs under License Number – B-1959/PUN/PART/1000+5/10127/2022. With more than a decade of expertise in the overseas consulting and immigration industry, Mr. Sharma has played a pivotal role in establishing Unique Overseas as a one-stop destination for manpower solutions, tourist visas, study visas, and permanent residency services. His visionary leadership and in-depth industry knowledge have enabled the company to expand its global presence, catering primarily to Gulf countries, Europe, Canada, and America. Respected for his professionalism, transparency, and client-focused approach, Mr. Sharma has earned the trust of thousands of individuals and businesses worldwide. Guided by ethical practices and a commitment to excellence, he continues to drive Unique Overseas toward innovation, sustainable growth, and long-term client success.

Our Objective

At Unique overseas our objective is to bridge opportunities across borders by providing reliable overseas consultancy services. We aim to simplify the journey of individuals and businesses seeking global growth—whether through skilled manpower supply, student admissions, or visa facilitation. Our focus is on delivering trusted guidance, transparent processes, and professional support for tourist, student, work, and permanent residency visas. We also strive to meet the workforce demands of international industries by connecting them with qualified, skilled, and dedicated professionals. With integrity, efficiency, and client satisfaction at the core, our mission is to be a trusted partner for individuals aspiring to build their future abroad and companies seeking a dependable global workforce

Our Mission

Our Mission Comprises of Meeting With our Client’s needs in The Most Relevant and Realistic Manner Where in we Provide Paramount Services along with a Commitment to Achieve that Transaction of Success.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to seamlessly connect individuals and businesses with quality travel experiences and skilled labour resources. Our aim is to become a trusted global leader in customized travel solutions, such as vacation packages, tours, and corporate travel arrangements.
